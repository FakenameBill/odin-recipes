Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies, just like mother used to make. These are more than a dessert. They are a memory. A striking reminder of the better times, when you, ignorant of the horrors of existence, still saw this world as a place of joy and wonder.

You may not be able to reverse the resolute, unending march of time and return to those halcyon days, but you can do the next best thing: stuff your face with chocolate chip cookies and let the taste of sugary nostalgia distract you from the harsh realities of life in the societal grist mill of a dying empire on a dying planet.

Your mileage may vary.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix together sugar and butter until smooth
  2. Mix in eggs one at a time
  3. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add along with salt
  4. Stir in flour and chocolate chips
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Place spoonfulls of batter on pan and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Enjoy the cookies and let the emotions flood in. It's okay to cry. Everyone cries.
