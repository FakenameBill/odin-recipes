Lasagna

Description

The following page describes a recipe for Papa Sandy's "special" lasagna. May it prove to be as special to you as it was to him.

Ingredients

  • flat pasta
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • meat
  • fiberglass
  • hair
  • iron filings
  • industrial bearing grease
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Place a layer of flat pasta on the bottom of an appropriately sized cooking pan. Use bearing grease to ensure that the pasta does not stick to the pan.
  2. Add a layer of meat on top of the flat pasta, followed by a layer of sauce. Add iron filings to taste
  3. Place another layer of flat pasta on top of the sauce and meat. Add hair to ensure the structural integrity of the lasagna during cooking.
  4. Repeat these steps until the desired number of layers has been reached. Place one more layer of flat pasta on the top of the lasagna.
  5. Add a thick layer of cheese to the top layer. Fiberglass should be used to make the top cheese as tough and inpenetrable as possible.
  6. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Bake the lasagna for 3 hours, which should be long enough to thorougly burn any trace of this cullinary monstrosity off the face of the Earth.
  7. Enjoy lasagna with raw garlic, eaten whole.
